⚡ Prediction Mode
Click ⚡ Predict in the header to enter prediction mode. The dashboard border turns gold.
On any unplayed match card, use ＋ / − buttons to set the home and away score.
Group tables, the 3rd-place leaderboard, and the knockout bracket all cascade and update live as you enter scores.
Cards with a predicted score show a PRED tag and a blue left border.
Click 📊 Real to switch back to official scores at any time — your predictions are preserved.
🎲 Auto-fill
Available only in Prediction Mode. Generates statistically-weighted random results for all unplayed matches.
Runs one match at a time over ~1 minute so you can watch the group tables, bracket, and standings animate in real time.
Automatically pauses between rounds (Group Stage → R32 → R16 → QF → SF → Final). A prompt lets you review each round before continuing.
Click ⏸ Pause mid-round to pause manually; ▶ Resume to continue from where you left off.
Win probability is weighted by FIFA rankings — higher-ranked teams win more often, but upsets happen.
